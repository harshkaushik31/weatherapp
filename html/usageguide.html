How to search for your desired city

  • 1. Go to the search bar
    If you are using your mobile click the three lines displayed on the top right corner of your smart phone.
  • 2. Go to the search tab
  • 3. Type the name of the city you want to search for and tap the serch button.
  • 4. Congratulations! You got the data for the weather of the city you were searching for.
